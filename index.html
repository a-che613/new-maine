MAX'S MAINE COONS

Breeder of European style Maine Coon cats located in Tweed, Ontario, Canada

Maine Coon's are sweet-tempered and gentle breeds. They are Large, Intelligent, Affectionate Cats who give a lot of Love to their parents and adapt to any environment.

About Us

Max'sCoones is a family business that has been established for nearly two decades, focused solely on producing gorgeous, family-friendly, DNA-healthy Maine coons. We have a thorough vetting process and re-home our Maine coons for their lifetime.
